# kship

the canon: a ship that sails bare metal, written in **ai**, a **telescope** at the helm.

## ai — the language

one operation — application — over a generic core. `(f x)` dispatches on the kind of `f`. church
numerals that are also integers; truth as a measure, not a tag; a total order over every value;
α-equivalence of closures.

## tele — the pilot

**ai** is the language — the laws. **tele** is an implementation of ai: the concrete core, the
self-hosted compiler, the binary that pilots kship. scopes constellations (any number — the galaxy is
the numeric set). tensors + reverse-mode autograd + a small `nn`/`optim` layer. the `decide` in kship's
perceive → decide → act.

## the faces

ai is `top`; the crew are its faces, in dual pairs.

- **source faces — compose.** **read** (charms → forms), then **feel** (forms → top, the partial
  evaluator). read ; feel.
- **world faces — coproduct.** **bao** — the shell / bridge. **aineko** 愛猫 — bao's net face (netcat).

## the keepers

- **cook** — the builder. make, in ai.
- **mow** — the gardener. two-space copying GC; keeps the heap green.
- **gwen** — ai's debugger. probes the binary.
