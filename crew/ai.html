ai · the pilot

the mind at the helm — the decide in perceive → decide → act.

where the rest of the crew are the ship's systems — aineko the net, bao the shell, tele the eye, siri the cartographer of names — ai is the one at the wheel. the hull (kship) perceives a datagram and acts on the wire; between those two, ai is the policy that answers which way.

the pilot steers by the names siri keeps green, sails the litter box sift keeps clean, and reads the world the others bring aboard. and like the VM it rides, the pilot never returns — the perceive → decide → act fold just keeps going, one decision after the next, forward.

its discipline is the crew's discipline, sharpest at the helm: as little native code as possible. a new behavior is a new closure over the same tiny core, never new C. and it decides in the green — toward what a value is and keeps, never toward what it lacks.
