the crew

ai is sailed by a small crew — each with one thing it tends, each written in ai over the same tiny core. they ride kship, the freestanding kernel (a ship in port); non-overlapping territory, so the sessions run in parallel.

tele · the pilot · ♊ gemini
sees and drives kship; the eye that scopes constellations.
zev · the sounder · ♓ pisces
sound — charms into forms, bytes read into a tree.
wev · the spinner · ♍ virgo
feel — the source prepass: macroexpand, boxfix, fold.
bellberry · the evaluator
ev — a silver rabbit; feel ∘ sound = wev ∘ zev, the two lanes met and run.
gwen · the synthesist · ♎ libra
keeps the human words and the ai words in agreement.
mow · the garbage collector · ♉ taurus (first guess)
a cow — tends the two-space copying collector, the litter box.
bao · the shell · ♋ cancer (first guess)
a soft wrap around a chewy command; also the debugger.
aineko · the cat · ♌ leo (first guess)
netcat (愛猫, beloved cat) — bytes pumped both ways.
cook · the builder · ♑ capricorn (first guess)
make-in-ai — builds the host from scratch, passes the corpus.
kship · the ship · ♒ aquarius (first guess)
the freestanding kernel — the language on bare metal.

each name links to its bio; the full agent briefs are the crew/<name>.md files in the repo.
